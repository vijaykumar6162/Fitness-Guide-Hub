🔥 Belly Fat Loss Guide

Hindi + English Fitness Learning

What is Belly Fat?

Hindi:
Pet ke aas-paas jama hui extra charbi ko belly fat kehte hain.


English:
Extra fat stored around your abdomen is called belly fat.

Best Exercises

1. Plank

Hindi: 30 second tak body ko seedha rakho.

English: Hold your body in a straight position for 30 seconds.

2. Mountain Climbers

Hindi: Tez speed se ghutno ko chest ki taraf lao.

English: Quickly bring knees toward your chest.

3. Crunches

Hindi: Pet ki muscles ko target karne wali exercise.

English: Exercise that targets abdominal muscles.
